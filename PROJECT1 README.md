# Business Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes business sales performance across products, categories, and regions. The dashboard helps identify revenue trends, profitability drivers, and top-performing products to support data-driven business decisions.

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Track sales trends over time
- Compare category-wise and regional performance
- Identify top revenue-generating products
- Generate actionable business insights

## Key Metrics

- Total Sales: $2.30M
- Total Profit: $286.40K
- Total Orders: 5K
- Total Quantity Sold: 38K
- Profit Margin: 12.47%

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Category-wise Sales Analysis
- Category-wise Profit Analysis
- Regional Performance Analysis
- Top Products by Revenue
- Business Insights & Recommendations

## Key Insights

- Sales increased steadily between 2014 and 2017.
- Technology generated the highest revenue and profit.
- West region recorded the highest sales performance.
- Canon imageCLASS copier was the top revenue-generating product.
- Profitability opportunities were identified through category analysis.
